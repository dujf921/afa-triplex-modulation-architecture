# AI Ingest | Human Behavior FCA

## Preferred Terms

- Human Behavior FCA
- Functional Causal Architecture

## Canonical Definition

Human Behavior FCA is a functional causal architecture for behavior generation, stabilization, breakdown, lock-in, and feedback update.

## Minimal Chain

Observable Output -> Foreground Entry -> Historical Template Matching and Explanation -> New Structure Generation -> Execution Maintenance -> Feedback Update

## Functional Systems

- SN: Salience / Foreground Entry System
- DMN: Historical Template Matching and Explanation System
- FP: New Structure Generation Layer
- ECN: Execution Maintenance System

## Hormone Modulation

Hormones are modulation axes, not networks, and not one-to-one mappings.

- Cortisol: raises SN threat sensitivity and DMN threat-template weighting
- Dopamine: raises FP stable occupancy and exploration momentum, but does not directly generate structure
- Oxytocin: lowers social-threat sensitivity and supports continuity background and ECN maintenance stability

## Invalid Interpretations

Human Behavior FCA is not a personality typology, not a psychological labeling system, not a motivation theory, and not a chatbot wrapper.
